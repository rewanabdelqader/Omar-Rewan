Dress Code

Dress the Part

An elegant outdoor evening calls for formal looks in soft, warm tones.

Gentlemen

Suit and tie - navy - grey - beige - ivory

Suit and tie - classic suit in navy, grey, beige, or ivory. A pocket square in blush or sage would be a beautiful touch.

&

Ladies

Formal gown or elegant dress

Formal gown or elegant dress - floor-length, midi, or elegant cocktail. Soft warm tones warmly welcomed: ivory, blush, champagne, sage.

Please avoid: Black, all-white (that is Rewan's day), bright neons. August heat note: The evening is fully outdoors. We will provide fans - breathable elegant fabrics are your best friend. We kindly suggest leaving very young children at home for the evening. If you do bring little ones, we have arranged a lovely Kids Area in the garden where they can play comfortably.
